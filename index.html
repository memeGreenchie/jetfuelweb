Revolutionizing GameFi with Play 2 Travel

JetFuel is a blockchain-based flight game where players earn JTF tokens through exciting aerial battles.

Unlike traditional Play-to-Earn (P2E) models, JetFuel integrates a real-world economy, allowing players to redeem tokens for flights, hotels, and travel services in the upcoming JetFuel Marketplace (2026).

Key Features of JetFuel (JTF)

Earn While You Play
Get JTF tokens as in-game rewards and use them for real-world travel.
Instant on Telegram
No downloads needed, play seamlessly within Telegram.
Play-to-Travel Model
Redeem JTF tokens for airline tickets, hotels, and travel services
Competitive Gameplay
Experience fast-paced aerial battles, leaderboards, and tournaments.
Sustainable Tokenomics
Balanced tokenomics and gradual emissions to ensure long-term value.
No NFTs Required
100% token-based rewards, making it simple and accessible for all players.
Travel Marketplace
A dedicated JetFuel Marketplace where JTF tokens will power real travel experiences.

Fast-Paced Flight Combat in Your Hands

Experience high-speed aerial battles in JetFuel’s Telegram mini-app game. Compete in PvP dogfights, complete missions, dominate the leaderboards—all while earning JTF tokens!
